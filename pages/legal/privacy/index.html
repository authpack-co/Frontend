AuthPack is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how the AuthPack browser extension and platform handle data, what we collect, and why.

01Information We Collect

AuthPack may store authentication session data locally in the user's browser to enable session restoration and automation across supported services. AuthPack does not collect or store user passwords.

Some information may be transmitted securely to AuthPack servers to enable extension functionality:

  • Account identifiers (e.g. Google user ID)
  • Package metadata created or acquired by the user
  • Device connection information for registered devices

02How We Use Information

Information is used solely to operate the AuthPack service. This includes:

  • Enabling session restoration on user request
  • Managing packages and access keys
  • Maintaining registered device connections
  • Improving reliability and performance of the extension

AuthPack does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

03Local Data Storage

Sensitive session data is stored locally within the browser environment and may be encrypted to protect user security. This data is used exclusively to perform session restoration when explicitly requested by the user.

04User Consent

By installing and using the AuthPack extension, users acknowledge and consent to the data processing described in this policy. Users retain full control over when sessions are created, restored, or removed.

05Third-Party Services

AuthPack only interacts with third-party websites when the user explicitly accesses those services through the extension. AuthPack is not responsible for the privacy practices of external platforms.

06Data Security

AuthPack implements technical safeguards to protect stored information and prevent unauthorized access, including encryption of locally stored session data where applicable.

07Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of AuthPack after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
